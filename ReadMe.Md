The player takes on the role of a hero whose mission is to rescue a kidnapped princess from a dragon. Along the way, the player must battle enemies, manage health, and make strategic decisions to survive.


The player fights enemies in turn-based combat
Actions include:
Attack to deal damage
Defend to reduce incoming damage
Use Potion to restore health
Enemies include:
Goblins (early levels)
Dragon (final boss)


Encapsulation – Character stats and properties are managed within classes
Inheritance – Player, Goblin, and Dragon inherit from a base Character class
Polymorphism – Actions (Attack, Defend, Use Item) use method overriding
Abstraction – Action and Item classes provide generalized behavior for extensions


Objective:
Defeat all enemies, survive through all levels, and ultimately defeat the Dragon to win the game.
